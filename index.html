Spreading Awareness and Protection of Endangered Species

Endangered species are species that are at a high risk of extinction. This advocacy works to defend, strengthen, fund, and ensure effective implementation of the Endangered Species Act and other core wildlife protection laws.

Species are the building blocks of a thriving ecosystem. Spreading its awareness and protection will allow our environment and ecosystem to fluorish and prevents a continuous destruction chain that will further destroy the quality and state of our own ecosystem. With this advocacy, you are not only helping the ecosystem, but also yourself.
